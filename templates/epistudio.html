About

I was thinking about this project since 2023, as I always wanted to have a development studio, especially a video-game studio. In 2025, I founded Epi studio to create Open-Source programs.

Challenges

The first challenge I faced was the recruitments: I had to convince people to join a group and to work for free, knowing their work is not theirs. I then had to find ideas about what to develop and create, while still maintaining the Open-Source first policy. I then had to figure out everything finance related: taxes, contract, partnerships, bank accounts, etc.

Solutions

For the recruitments, I chose the most motivated people, this was my criteria. Agains't all thoughts, I had a lot of candidates who were ready to create and to be involved ! I asked each candidate about their relationship to Open-Source, their experience and their motivations. When someone create something in the studio, their creation is automatically the studio's one, but they still have all the rights as long as they don't make others pay, which builds trust with our studio. Every time I submit an Idea to the group, I think about three things: Will it be usefull for someone, will this interest my team and be fun to build, and finally can this project be Open-Source ? If it fits, its submitted to vote, if it doesn't fit, it goes to trash!

Future

EPI Studio has a bright future! I have no plan on making money out of it: its only reason to be is to build, create and share, for free. In the future, I want us to start building games, like a big studio!
